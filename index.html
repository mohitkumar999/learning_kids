🔒 Privacy Policy

Safe, Secure, and Fun!

At Kids Learn Hub, your child's safety is our #1 priority. Here is how we protect you:

  • No Personal Data: We do not ask for real names, addresses, or phone numbers.
  • COPPA Compliant: We follow all rules of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act.
  • Safe Breaks Only: The screens you see between games are completely safe and built-in just for fun pacing. We do not track you.
  • No Chat Rooms: There is no way for strangers to talk to your child on our platform.

If you have any questions, parents can email us at safety@kidslearnhub.com.
